# Kampung Iman — Zones and Premium Low-Poly Art Direction

## Respectful visual rules

- Do **not** depict prophets, angels, sahabah, or sacred historical figures.
- Avoid visible faces. Use back-view children, featureless rounded heads, small silhouettes, or hands-only interaction icons.
- Keep clothing modest: loose tunics, long sleeves, long skirts/trousers, soft hijab shapes where appropriate, no body-emphasis.
- Avoid writing Qur'an verses as decorative texture on floors/walls. If Arabic appears, place it only in readable UI lesson cards with source labels and parent-review copy.
- Mosque spaces should be calm, clean, and symbolic: domes, arches, lanterns, prayer rugs without people performing detailed ritual animations.
- No combat, fear, demons, magic, or caricatured religious imagery.

## Premium playful low-poly style

- **Shape language:** rounded low-poly forms, bevelled edges, chunky readable silhouettes, toy-diorama proportions.
- **Camera feel:** WebGL-friendly 3/4 isometric or soft third-person orbit with shallow parallax; avoid claustrophobic interiors.
- **Materials:** matte clay/plaster walls, warm wood, woven rugs, glazed ceramic accents, soft water shader, leaf cards with hand-painted gradients.
- **Lighting:** golden-hour ambient light, soft blob shadows, gentle bloom only on collectibles/lanterns, no harsh neon.
- **Palette:** warm sand, date-palm green, turquoise tile, cream plaster, terracotta roofs, night-sky indigo for quiet reflection areas.
- **Animation:** collectibles bob slowly; leaves sway; lanterns pulse; UI cards flip like sturdy paper; reward stars sparkle softly, not explosively.
- **Performance target:** low-poly modular kits, instanced foliage/lanterns/books, atlas-based UI icons, baked ambient occlusion, optional dynamic shadows only under player and interactables.

## Zone theme 1: Kindness Village

**Learning focus:** good speech, respect for parents/caregivers, helping neighbors, removing harm from pathways.

**Key landmarks**
- Small mosque silhouette with dome and minaret in background, not a detailed ritual scene.
- Courtyard homes with modest doorways, clay pots, laundry lines, date palms.
- Market path with baskets, bread crates, water jars, a clear safe walkway.
- Community notice board for quiz prompts and good-deed stickers.

**Interactive props**
- Banana peel or fallen branch pickup for "remove harm" lesson.
- Kind Words Lantern collectible near a neighbor's doorstep.
- Helping Hand Token near a repaired path stone.
- Speech-bubble UI icon with no character faces.

**Mood target:** welcoming, safe, sunlit, neighborly; the first zone should feel like a playable toy village.

## Zone theme 2: Garden of Mercy

**Learning focus:** mercy, care for creation, diversity, patience, hope after difficulty.

**Key landmarks**
- Walled garden with geometric paths, fountains, flowerbeds, beehive boxes, birdhouses.
- Animal-care corner with water bowls and shade canopies; avoid anthropomorphic animals that mock religious themes.
- Mountain-path backdrop for the hardship/ease lesson, using a gentle slope and sunrise reveal.

**Interactive props**
- Mercy Seed planted into a flowerbed; blooms after a correct answer.
- Respect Flower showing varied colors in one garden bed.
- Patience Cloud drifting above a retry puzzle.
- Watering-can mini interaction for caring actions.

**Mood target:** lush, calm, compassionate; reward children for noticing small needs in the environment.

## Zone theme 3: House of Knowledge Library

**Learning focus:** seeking knowledge, using time well, speaking good or staying silent.

**Key landmarks**
- Cozy library/madrasa-inspired reading room with arches, shelves, rugs, cushions, ink pots, globe-like geometric model.
- Clock-tower nook for Surah Al-'Asr time lesson.
- Parent/teacher review desk represented by books and a lamp, no authority figure needed.

**Interactive props**
- Knowledge Star collectible above an open book stand.
- Sand timer or low-poly clock puzzle for time stewardship.
- Quiet Corner sign for "speak good or remain silent" reflection.
- Source-card shelf: each quiz unlocks a labeled card with Qur'an/hadith reference.

**Mood target:** curious, cozy, premium educational; make reading feel like treasure hunting.

## UI and collectible presentation

- Use large rounded cards with icon, lesson title, one-sentence child explanation, and small source line.
- Prefer icons over faces: lantern, book, seed, flower, path stone, cloud, clock, speech bubble.
- For correct answers: warm chime, card glow, collectible sparkle, short affirmation such as "MashaAllah, kind choice!"
- For incorrect answers: no buzzer or shame; use "Try again" hints and highlight the relevant environmental prop.
- Add a parent-facing source drawer listing every Qur'an/hadith reference used by the current level.

## Asset kit checklist

- Modular village: plaster wall, arch doorway, dome silhouette, minaret silhouette, roof tile, market stall, path stone.
- Garden: palm, bush, flower clusters, fountain, water bowl, birdhouse, seed mound, mountain backdrop.
- Library: bookshelf modules, book stacks, reading stand, rug, cushion, lantern, clock/sand timer, source-card shelf.
- Collectibles: Kind Words Lantern, Knowledge Star, Mercy Seed, Helping Hand Token, Patience Cloud, Respect Flower.
- UI icons: Qur'an reference card, sahih hadith card, retry arrow, parent review badge, audio narration button.
